Our Company

Art Asia limited dedicates to promote oriental art and culture worldwide. Based in the City of London surrounded by top museums, auction houses and art institutions, Art Asia also has branches in Boston, Hong Kong and Mumbai. With access to multicultural art markets and a well-structured business network, our experts and specialists work to provide professional and trustworthy services including publishing, exhibition planning, art advisory, art investment for both individuals and organisations. Over the years, Art Asia has expanded into art markets including Chinese, Japanese, Indian, and South-East Asia.

Art Asia has built up and maintained a good relationship with domestic and international museums, libraries, academic institutions, scholars, and private collectors. With great market insights and differentiated methodologies, our strategic teams have successfully developed business into media, publishing and education including copyright importing, subject selection, translation and edit, catalogueuing, and academic communication. Art Asia meanwhile cooperates with top universities and major Chinese art media, like magazine "World Heritage", "Collector World" and internal magazines of majors banks for VIP clients to provide Asian art collectors the latest and authentic art reviews.
