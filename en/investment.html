Art Investment

Over the last 25 years, the return of art investment has gradually outperformed the returns of bonds and equity, closely following the returns of real estates and commodity investment. Noticeably, since 2008, the return of Chinese antiques has achieved the best among all Chinese artworks with the highest return.

Art Asia Limited aims to operate under the international art investment norms to proivde the professional and accurate investment services and trend analysis of art markets, artworks and artists for investment institutions and individuals, collectors and people from other art circles.

Art Asia Art Funds deploy investment models for both institutional and private investors to establish investment portfolios in the art markets to achieve risk-return optimisation and long-term capital gains while in compliance with regulations and conducts. Meanwhile, Art Asia Art Funds offer investment start-ups, investment diversification, risk management, and investment exit advisory services.
