  • (Chinese Ceramics: Highlights of the Sir Percival David Collection)-Chinese Version

    Nov 2013 - First Chinese version worldwide - Limited copies
    Room 95 of the British Museum opened to the public in 2009 to display the collection of Sir Percival David. Sir Percival David spent more than forty years collecting and most of the pieces belong to his own collection. As a result of Sir Percival David's lifelong passion and dedication to Chinese art, this collection is one of the finest Chinese porcelain collections worldwide, compares favourably to the Taipei National Palace Museum ceramic collection and the Beijing Palace Museum ceramic collection. This book has highlighted 50 groups of ceramics from almost 1,700 objects of this world-renowned collection.

    This book is the limited Chinese version of hardback edition of "Chinese Ceramics: Highlights of the Sir Percival David Collection". The authors of this book are the most prestigious international scholar Regina Krahl and Jessica Harrison-Hall, the Curator, Department of Asia, British Museum and four top Chinese ceramaic renowned scholars as translators and proofreader.
